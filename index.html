Meet Shantel

Shantel is a "Jackie" of all Trades! She's a Creative! Whether it's crafting, baking, or designing, she loves to create with those magical hands. Being her own boss has always been a dream of hers.

She has successfully established two small businesses, PHAT GIRLZ SWEETS & MORE and LE SHANT RENE' DESIGNS. When she isn't busy creating, Shantel is heading disaster/economic recovery through her non-profit, REVIVE LOUISIANA VOLUNTEER PROJECT. Through this organization, she is able to assist those in need with food, employment services, social services, and more.

Currently, Shantel is trying her hand at a new love, Computer Science. She is attempting to attain skills that will assist her in additional streams of revenue to support her non-profit initiative. She will learn Coding through Front-End Development.
